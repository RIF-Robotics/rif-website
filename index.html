Robotics for Hospital Logistics
Tell Me More

Solutions

We write software for robots to help people.

Case Cart Assembling Robot

We are developing a mobile robot that can assemble surgical case carts in a hospital without modifying the existing infrastructure.

Hospital Inventory Metrics

Our system automatically tracks and records the conditions of all surgical tools and supplies as it assembles each surgical case cart.

Human Robot Collaboration

Our robots are designed to safely work in collaboration with healthcare workers by constantly monitoring for the presence of people. This means that hospital managers do not have to modify their existing hospital infrastructure to begin leveraging our case cart assembling robot.
